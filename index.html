Acoustic Live Music

Andante is a premium acoustic covers duo providing quality live entertainment for pubs, clubs, weddings and functions.

Based in the heart of Torbay, our duo specialises in delivering melodic performances that span the decades, from the swinging 60s to today's chart-topping hits. With a repertoire comprising of rock, pop, and soul classics, reworked and reimagined for acoustic guitar and harmonised vocals.

Jon / Guitar

Jon is an experienced player and has played guitar in various bands and duos throughout Devon and London.

He has always had an interest in the technical aspects of music and guitar, drawing inspiration from guitarists such as The Edge of U2, Francis Dunnery of It Bites and David Rhodes from the Peter Gabriel band. He has a keen interest in sound engineering and can often be found working in his home studio in Newton Abbot.
